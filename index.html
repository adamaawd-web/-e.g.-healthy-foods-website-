Whole Grains & Legumes

  • Whole grains: oats, brown rice, barley, quinoa, buckwheat, whole wheat pasta, millet, amaranth, bulgur, spelt
  • Legumes: lentils, chickpeas, black beans, kidney beans, navy beans, lima beans, edamame

Protein

  • Nuts: almonds, walnuts
  • Seeds: chia, hemp, flaxseeds, pumpkin, sunflower
  • Nut and seed butters: peanut butter, tahini
  • Fatty fish: salmon, sardines, mackerel, tuna, herring
  • Eggs
  • Lean meats: chicken, turkey, grass-fed beef
  • Dairy and fermented dairy: Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, kefir

Healthy Junk Food Recipes

Healthy Burger

Whole wheat buns, lean patty, fresh veggies, low-fat sauces. Grill or bake.

Healthy Fries

Bake sweet potato or potato fries with herbs instead of oil.

Healthy Pizza

Whole grain base, tomato sauce, veggies, low-fat cheese, lean protein optional.

Healthy Snacks

Baked veggie chips, air-popped popcorn, fruit-based treats.

Healthy Sweets

Frozen yogurt, banana ice cream, baked donuts/cakes, minimal sugar.
